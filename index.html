NEIGHBOURHOOD

Westlake

Westlake is an area located at west gate of UTAR and and had been housing over thousands of tenants near to University Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR) and Kolej Tunku Abdul Rahman (KTAR). Westlake is also popular known as Westlake Homes for the locals.

Read More About Westlake

Westlake Lakeside Garden

Located beside Westlake residential, there is a lakeside garden allows students or tenants to relax themselves. This garden will provide a view from lakeside to the UTAR Grand Hall and UTAR Block A. Besides lakeside view, this garden also providing a jogging track for student to exercise to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Visit Website

University Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR)

Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman Main Campus is one of the most prestigious universities in Malaysia with having over 15 thousands of students. The campus in Kampar was built on a 1300-acre piece of land donated by the Perak State Government. It is located in the growing township of Bandar Barat Kampar in Perak. It is only 600m away from Westlake Residential.

Visit UTAR Website

Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology (TAR UMT)

TAR UMT is one of the oldest institutions of higher learning in Malaysia with more than 190,000 students having passed through its doors. The second campus were founded in Kampar, Perak in 1998. It is only 400m distance between TAR UMT & Westlake Residential.

Visit TAR UMT Website

MMCineplexes

MMCineplexes Kampar located at the old town Kampar with 5 stories building. The cinema is one of the attraction where allow students to spend their quality resting time with the latest released movies. MMCineplexes Kampar housing at least 5 2D cinema and 1 3D cinema with a total of 6 cinema in the building. The cinema able to accommodate total of 836 customers at the same time.

Visit Website

Starbucks Coffee

Starbucks Coffee were built in Kampar since year 2015 and is located in Tin Village or beside econsave super market in New Town. Starbucks Coffee Kampar is one of the favourite places for students or tenants to chit-chat or relax themselves with premium coffee or chocolate drinks.

Visit Website

McDonald's, KFC, Domino & Subway

All the fast food like McDonald’s, KFC, Domino & Subway are located at the same area, Taman Bandar Baru Kampar. It is only 4 mins away from Westlake Residential. It is very convenient for students to solve their worry of food hunting.

Econsave

The only hypermarket located in the Westlake area. Buildings were located in Tin Village. Econsave was established on early 2015 and had been one of the most visited hypermarket due to the short distance for students or tenants to visit it from Westlake.

Lotus
